Welcome to Bordtenniskameratene! The most competitive sports group of Leonardo Linjeforening. As we all know, Table tennis is a serious sport. From breakrooms in corporate buildings to the biggest arenas in the Olympic games, table tennis captures the competitive instinct of the entire world. Bordtennikameratene is a group for the students at Leonardo linjeforening who wants to have fun, make friends and improve their topspin. We welcome everyone to our group. Regardless of skill.
