Welcome to Leonardo Fk! the official football club for students at industrial design. We are a low threshold club with one team for women and one team for men. Every Monday at 16:30 both teams practice together in Dødens dal. Usually, the practice lasts until 18:00. We mainly play seven a side football in the “Sjudent” student-league but during the winter we also compete in a futsal league. Leonardo FK is known for great parties, our official drink “Leobombs” and an inclusive environment. Everyone is always welcome to join our team regardless of their past experience. So if you have never played football before or you are a retired professional, please feel free to join our team. We can guarantee a good time and many unforgettable moments!
